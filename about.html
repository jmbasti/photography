About

Journey in Photos… Just a typical photo hobbyist… started when my brother lend me his camera, started to know my way around it. Though the images were not as sharp, the curiosity then started.

 

Though what I finished in school is extremes of what I love the most, I was still able to adapt on the field of photography. Along the years, through self study and practice, I was able to capture images that appealed not only to me what but to the others. The curiosity then became a hobby and eventually now… a passion. Whatever that appeals me, I shoot. Capturing the emotions, the finesse, the scenery, and the beauty of it all.

 

My friend & mentor through his initiative gave me some important tips and the thing that stuck into my mind was; “ Your photo must tell a story…”

 

Browse the page for my various images…. Happy viewing.
